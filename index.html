About

The Human Behind the Code

I'm Brishav Rajbahak — a developer forged in the chaotic, electric energy of Kathmandu, where century-old temples share skylines with satellite dishes. That contrast — ancient meets digital, chaos meets precision — is wired into everything I build.

I obsess over the millisecond a page loads, the exact easing curve of an animation, the architecture decision nobody notices until the app survives a traffic spike. The details aren't the finishing touch — they are the product.

When the terminal is closed, you'll find me deep inside an RPG, reverse-engineering why the world feels alive, or pacing the room because I can't stop thinking about the next build.
